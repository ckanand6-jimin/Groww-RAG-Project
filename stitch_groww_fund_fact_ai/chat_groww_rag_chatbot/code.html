Can you explain the exit load structure for HDFC Balanced Advantage Fund if I redeem within 6 months?

smart_toy

For the **HDFC Balanced Advantage Fund**, the exit load structure is applied based on the duration of your holding and the amount redeemed:

  • Up to 15% of the units: No exit load is applicable if redeemed within 1 year from the date of allotment.
  • More than 15% of the units: An exit load of 1.00% is applicable if redeemed within 1 year.
  • After 1 year: Nil exit load.

Since you are asking about a 6-month timeframe, if you redeem more than 15% of your initial investment, a 1% exit load will be deducted.
